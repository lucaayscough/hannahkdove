Hi, I’m Hannah. I am a photographer and collage artist living in Scotland.

I have been featured in i-D Magazine, The Skinny, The Glasgow Gallery of Photography, and public slideshows at the renowned Rencontres d’Arles. I was listed for the Scottish Portrait Award in 2023. Much is still to come.

I work by day at a cafe so that I can pay rent and fund my artistic practice. If you like what you saw here, please consider supporting my passion by donating to my Patreon or buying one of my pieces. Every little bit helps me keep doing what I love. :)
